How to play:

Your goal is to pick between the green and red arrows. Green means higher, red means lower. The card on the left is yours, and you're up against the card on the right. The key? Think of the Pokémon's Dex number! Compare them: for example, if it's Rotom (Dex 479) vs. Marshadow (Dex 802), you'd hit red for lower. Each time you guess, your opponent's card becomes yours. But there’s a twist—you only get three chances before losing all your points. Oh, and you're racing against the clock, so no peeking at PokéWiki!
